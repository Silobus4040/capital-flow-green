    ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
        Q: How is DSCR calculated and what does it mean?
A:
 DSCR = Net Operating Income ÷ Annual Debt Service. A 1.25 DSCR means the property generates 25% more income than needed to cover the loan payment, providing a safety cushion.
        Q: Do you require personal income verification?
A:
 No! DSCR loans qualify based solely on the property's cash flow performance. We don't require tax returns, W2s, or personal income documentation.
        Q: Can I finance multiple properties in one loan?
A:
 Yes! Our portfolio program allows you to finance 2-50+ properties in a single transaction with cross-collateralization benefits and streamlined processing.
        Q: What if my property is partially vacant?
A:
 We underwrite to both in-place income and pro forma rents. Partial vacancy is acceptable with appropriate DSCR adjustments and potential reserve requirements.
        Q: Are there prepayment penalties?
A:
 We offer flexible prepayment options including step-down schedules (5-4-3-2-1 or 3-2-1) and no-penalty options with slight rate adjustments for 10+ year terms.
